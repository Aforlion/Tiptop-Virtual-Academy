# 🧒 Student Journey — Testing Guide
### Tiptop Virtual Academy | Prepared by JBK Technologies

---

> [!NOTE]
> **How to access the Student View**
>
> The student dashboard is accessed through the **Parent account**.
> 1. Log in as a **Parent**
> 2. Go to the **Parent Dashboard**
> 3. Find your child's profile card
> 4. Click **"Enter Student Dashboard"**
>
> The screen will automatically change style based on your child's age.

---

## 🎨 The Three Student Themes

Tiptop Virtual Academy has three different looks — one for each age group:

| Age Group | What it Looks Like |
|---|---|
| **Junior (3–6 years)** | Bright and bubbly! Cream background, orange and pink colours, big round buttons |
| **Senior (7–12 years)** | Cool and spacey! Dark background with cyan and blue sci-fi theme |
| **Teen (13–16 years)** | Clean and professional! Dark workspace with purple and indigo tones |

## ✅ Student Testing Checklist

---

### 🌈 Step 1 — Test the Junior Dashboard (Ages 3–6)

*To test this, your child's age must be between 3 and 6. Add a child with the right date of birth from the parent dashboard, or ask your administrator.*

- [ ] **1.1** Enter the student dashboard for a **young child (age 3–6)**.
- [ ] **1.2** ✅ Confirm the screen looks **bright, cheerful, and playful** — cream/white background with pink and orange colours.
- [ ] **1.3** ✅ Confirm the buttons are **very large and rounded** (pill-shaped).
- [ ] **1.4** ✅ Confirm there is a **bouncing emoji avatar** (🐹 or similar) at the top of the page.
- [ ] **1.5** ✅ Confirm the **upcoming class card** uses friendly language like *"Your Playroom is Ready!"*
- [ ] **1.6** Find the **Check-In button** (large orange button) and click it.
  - ✅ Confirm you enter the **Classroom Playground Lobby**.
  - ✅ Confirm you can see a list of classmates with a green "In Lobby" status next to some.
- [ ] **1.7** Click **"Play Test Bell"** in the lobby.
  - ✅ Confirm you hear a short **ding-dong chime** sound.

---

### 🚀 Step 2 — Test the Senior Dashboard (Ages 7–12)

*To test this, your child's age must be between 7 and 12.*

- [ ] **2.1** Enter the student dashboard for a **child aged 7–12**.
- [ ] **2.2** ✅ Confirm the screen looks **dark, futuristic, and space-themed** — dark background with a cyan/blue grid and glowing highlights.
- [ ] **2.3** ✅ Confirm the heading says **"Nexus Learner Terminal"**.
- [ ] **2.4** ✅ Confirm the avatar glows slightly (a pulsing sci-fi effect).
- [ ] **2.5** Scroll down to find the **XP and Gamification section**:
  - ✅ Confirm you can see an **XP bar** showing current points.
  - ✅ Confirm you can see **Challenges** (e.g. *"Attend 3 classes"*, *"Earn the Logic Wizard badge"*).
  - ✅ Confirm you can see a **Leaderboard** with student rankings.
- [ ] **2.6** Find the **Classroom section** — look for a pulsing "LIVE ROOM ACTIVE" indicator.
  - Click **"Signal Presence & Join Lobby"** (or equivalent button).
  - ✅ Confirm a lobby screen appears with classmates' status indicators.
  - ✅ Confirm a **countdown timer** is visible.
- [ ] **2.7** Wait for the countdown to reach zero (or come back when a session is live).
  - ✅ Confirm the **"Enter Live Assembly"** button becomes active and glows.

---

### 💻 Step 3 — Test the Teen Dashboard (Ages 13–16)

*To test this, your child's age must be between 13 and 16.*

- [ ] **3.1** Enter the student dashboard for a **teenager aged 13–16**.
- [ ] **3.2** ✅ Confirm the screen looks **clean and professional** — very dark background, no bright colours, indigo highlights.
- [ ] **3.3** ✅ Confirm the heading says **"Teen Workspace Terminal"**.
- [ ] **3.4** ✅ Confirm there are **no big gamification elements** — this screen looks more like a workspace.
- [ ] **3.5** ✅ Confirm the check-in button shows **developer-style text** (e.g. `npm run checkin`).
- [ ] **3.6** Click the check-in button.
  - ✅ Confirm the lobby shows **ping times** next to each classmate (e.g. *8ms, 12ms*) — a fun developer detail.

---

### 🧠 Step 4 — Taking a Quiz (Assessment)

*This tests the quiz engine. You can do this from any age group.*

- [ ] **4.1** Look for an **Assessments** link in the student navigation (or scroll down the dashboard).
- [ ] **4.2** Click on an available assessment.
  - ✅ Confirm the quiz page loads with a list of questions on the right side.
- [ ] **4.3** Answer all the questions:
  - For **multiple choice**, click the option you think is right.
  - For **fill-in-the-blank**, type your answer into the box.
  - For **reading comprehension**, read the passage carefully then choose your answer.
- [ ] **4.4** Use the **question navigation grid** (numbered buttons) to jump between questions.
  - ✅ Confirm visited questions are highlighted differently from unvisited ones.
- [ ] **4.5** Click **"Mark for Review"** on at least one question.
  - ✅ Confirm that question shows a different colour in the navigation grid.
- [ ] **4.6** Click **"Submit"** when you have answered all questions.
  - ✅ Confirm a **results page** appears showing:
    - Your percentage score (shown as a donut chart)
    - Number of **Correct**, **Incorrect**, and **Skipped** answers
    - A breakdown table of every question with your answer vs. the correct answer

---

### 🏆 Step 5 — Checking Gamification (Seniors only)

- [ ] **5.1** Scroll down the Senior dashboard to the **Gamification Centre**.
- [ ] **5.2** ✅ Confirm you can see your **current XP total** and a coloured progress bar.
- [ ] **5.3** ✅ Confirm you can see the **weekly streak calendar** showing which days you were active.
- [ ] **5.4** ✅ Confirm you can see at least one challenge with a progress bar (e.g. *"Class Explorer — Attend 3 sessions: 1/3"*).
- [ ] **5.5** ✅ Confirm you can see the **Leaderboard** tab with student rankings.

---

### 🎓 Step 6 — Viewing Certificates

- [ ] **6.1** Click **"Certificates"** in the student navigation.
- [ ] **6.2** ✅ Confirm the certificates page loads.
- [ ] **6.3** ✅ Confirm you can see any certificates earned for completed courses.
  - *(May be empty if no courses are completed yet — that is completely fine.)*
- [ ] **6.4** If a certificate exists, click on it and confirm you can **download it as a PDF**.

## 💡 Quick Tips

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| The screen looks plain/dark, not colourful | Make sure the child's date of birth is correct. Age 3–6 = Junior (bright), Age 7–12 = Senior (space), Age 13–16 = Teen (professional). |
| I don't hear the bell sound | Make sure your device's sound is not muted. Click "Play Test Bell" again. |
| The "Enter Session" button is grey/locked | The countdown is still running. The button unlocks when the timer reaches zero. |
| I can't find the Assessments section | Ask your administrator to add an assessment to a course first. |

## 🐞 Reporting Issues

If anything looks wrong or doesn't work, note:
1. Which **screen** you were on (e.g. *"Junior Dashboard — Classroom Lobby"*)
2. What **button you clicked**
3. What **happened** (error or nothing happened)
4. A **screenshot** if possible *(press Windows Key + Shift + S)*
