## 🗺️ Your Navigation Menu

Once you log in, you will automatically land on the **Teacher Console**. Your left sidebar has the following sections:

| Menu Item | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | See all your upcoming and completed sessions |
| **My Classes** | Manage individual class sessions — take attendance & award badges |
| **My Cohorts** | View the students enrolled in your term-based courses |
| **Availability** | Set the days and hours you are available to teach |
| **Lesson Plans** | Write and save notes/outlines for each of your classes |
| **My Earnings** | See a record of your pay for completed sessions |

## ✅ Teacher Testing Checklist

Go through each task in order. Tick them off as you complete them!

---

### 📋 Step 1 — First Login & Profile Check

- [ ] **1.1** Open the website and enter your email and temporary password.
- [ ] **1.2** When prompted, change your password to something you will remember.
- [ ] **1.3** ✅ Confirm you land on the **Teacher Console** (you should see a heading "Teacher Console").
- [ ] **1.4** ✅ Confirm the stats at the top show **Scheduled Classes**, **Classes Completed**, and your **Verified Instructor** badge.

---

### 🕐 Step 2 — Setting Your Availability

*This tells the school when you are free to teach.*

- [ ] **2.1** Click **"Availability"** in the left menu.
- [ ] **2.2** Add an availability block:
  - Choose a **day** (e.g. Monday)
  - Set a **start time** (e.g. 9:00 AM)
  - Set an **end time** (e.g. 11:00 AM)
  - Click **Save**.
  - ✅ Confirm the slot appears in your availability list.
- [ ] **2.3** Try to add a **second slot that overlaps** with the first one:
  - e.g. Monday 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM (overlaps with 9–11 AM)
  - ✅ Confirm the system **shows an error** and does NOT save the overlapping slot.
  - *(This is the scheduling protection working correctly!)*
- [ ] **2.4** Add a second, **non-overlapping** slot (e.g. Monday 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM).
  - ✅ Confirm it saves successfully.

---

### 📚 Step 3 — Writing a Lesson Plan

*Lesson plans help you stay organised and can be seen during class.*

- [ ] **3.1** Click **"Lesson Plans"** (or **Lessons**) in the left menu.
- [ ] **3.2** Click **"Create New Lesson Plan"**.
  - Select a course from the dropdown.
  - Add a title (e.g. *"Week 1: Introduction to Planets"*)
  - Write some notes in the content box (e.g. *"Cover Mercury, Venus, and Earth. Show the solar system model."*)
  - Click **Save**.
  - ✅ Confirm the lesson plan appears in your list.

---

### 👥 Step 4 — Viewing Your Cohort & Students

*Cohorts are fixed groups of students enrolled in your term courses.*

- [ ] **4.1** Click **"My Cohorts"** in the left menu.
- [ ] **4.2** ✅ Confirm you can see a list of courses you are assigned to.
- [ ] **4.3** Click on a course to expand it.
  - ✅ Confirm you can see the list of enrolled students.
  - ✅ Confirm students are filtered by the correct age range for that course.

---

### 🎥 Step 5 — Launching a Live Class

*When it is time for class, this is how you join.*

- [ ] **5.1** Go back to the **Dashboard** (click "Dashboard" in the menu).
- [ ] **5.2** Find an upcoming session in the **"Upcoming Assigned Live Rooms"** section.
- [ ] **5.3** Click the **"Open Live Room"** button next to a session.
  - ✅ Confirm a new browser tab opens with the Jitsi video meeting room.
  - *(You can close it immediately — this is just confirming it works.)*

---

### 🏅 Step 6 — Logging Attendance & Awarding Badges

*After each class, you record who attended and reward students.*

- [ ] **6.1** On the Dashboard, find a session and click **"Log Attendance & Badges"**.
- [ ] **6.2** ✅ Confirm you see a class management page with a student list.
- [ ] **6.3** Mark one or more students as **attended** by clicking the attendance toggle.
- [ ] **6.4** Award a badge to a student (e.g. 🧠 Logic Wizard or 🚀 Space Explorer).
  - ✅ Confirm the badge is saved against the student's name.

---

### 💰 Step 7 — Checking Your Earnings

*Your pay is automatically calculated when a class is marked as completed.*

- [ ] **7.1** Click **"My Earnings"** in the left menu.
- [ ] **7.2** ✅ Confirm you can see an earnings ledger table.
  - *(It may show empty if no sessions have been marked "completed" yet — that is fine!)*
- [ ] **7.3** ✅ Confirm you can see columns for: Session, Base Fee, Credit Share, Total, and Paid Out status.

3. What **happened** (error message or unexpected result)
