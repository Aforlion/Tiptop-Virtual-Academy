I asked the client how do you set up a school?

A typical school has Director/Proprietor, then Acdemic and non-Academic staff.

Acdemic staff includes, Head of School, who Head of departments reports to, and teachers reporting to Heads of departments.

Non-academic staff includes, Bursar (Accounting), HR, Marketer, Facility manager. They all report to the Admin.

The school will use The EYFS and Cambridge curricullum. These have learning areas and subjects. also, these curriculum have different stages of learning for children.  These stages are related to the age of the children.
For example Early years is for 3 to 5 year olds, primary is for 6 to 11 year olds, secondary is for 12 to 16 year olds. 

We will create classes accordingly, each class will have  a scheme of work, and a teacher will be assigned to the class. The teacher will create lesson plans for the class. 

In the case of EYFS, it has 7 areas of learning.  For each area of learning, there will be a scheme of work. 

In the case of Cambridge, it has subjects. For each subject, there will be a scheme of work. 

For Early years, the teacher will create lesson plans for each area of learning. 

For Primary and secondary, the teacher will create lesson plans for each subject. 

For online purpose the Director is also the admin. So there is only Admin, Head of school and teachers. 

So the roles will be, Admin, Head of school, teacher, parent, and student.
